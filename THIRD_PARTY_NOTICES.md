# Third-Party Notices

## idleon-data

HA Idleon vendors a small subset of class-name mapping data from
[`Corbeno/idleon-data`](https://github.com/Corbeno/idleon-data).

- Source file:
  [`maps/classNames.json`](https://github.com/Corbeno/idleon-data/blob/main/maps/classNames.json)
- Package metadata license: MIT
- Author: Corbeno

The vendored mapping is used to translate raw Idleon class identifiers into
display names.
